---

### Section 2: Future Directions

The field of Physical AI is moving faster than any other branch of technology. We are currently transitioning from **"Fixed Robots"** (factory arms) to **"General Purpose Humanoids"** (agents that can learn any task).

#### Key Trends
- **VLA Fine-Tuning**: Moving from general LLMs to models fine-tuned on millions of hours of robotic teleoperation data.
- **Embedded Supercomputing**: Higher-power AI chips (like NVIDIA Jetson Thor) becoming small enough to fit inside a robot's head.
- **Foundation Models for Motion**: AI models that understand the "Physics of Movement" as well as GPT understands the "Physics of Language."
